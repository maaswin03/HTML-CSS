IPL - Indian Premier League

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India. It is one of the most popular and richest cricket leagues in the world.Founded in 2008, IPL features franchise-based teams representing different cities. It combines sports, entertainment, and global talent.
There are currently 10 teams in the Indian Premier League (IPL): Chennai Super Kings (CSK), Mumbai Indians (MI), Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR), Delhi Capitals (DC), Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH), Rajasthan Royals (RR), Lucknow Super Giants (LSG), Gujarat Titans (GT), and Punjab Kings (PBKS).
Some top players in the Indian Premier League (IPL) include MS Dhoni, Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, AB de Villiers, Andre Russell, Jasprit Bumrah, and David Warner. These players have made significant contributions to their teams and have been fan favorites over the years.
